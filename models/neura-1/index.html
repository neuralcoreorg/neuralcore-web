About Neura 1

Neura 1 is our foundational large language model, designed to understand and generate human-like text across a wide variety of topics and use cases. With 175 billion parameters, it offers impressive capabilities in text completion, creative writing, and conversational interactions.

This model excels at adapting to different writing styles, following instructions, and maintaining context over longer exchanges. It can assist with drafting content, answering questions, brainstorming ideas, and more.
